Buttered Shrimp

Ingredients

  • Fresh Shrimp
  • Garlic
  • Butter
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Thaw shrimp.
  2. Mince garlic.
  3. Heat pan.
  4. Add butter
  5. Add garlic, fry until golden.
  6. Add shrimp, cook for 3 minutes each, flip after 1.5 minutes for even cooking
  7. Serve.
