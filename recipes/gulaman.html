Gulaman

Gulaman

Ingredients

  • Unflavorfed black or red gulaman.
  • Brown sugar
  • Banana flavoring
  • Ice

Steps

  1. Cook gulaman according to instructions. Set aside.
  2. Add 2 cups of sugar to 8 cups of boiling water. Cook until combined.
  3. Add 3 drops of banana flavoring.
  4. Turn off heat. Set aside to cool.
  5. Slice gulaman into cubes.
  6. In a tall glass, add the syrup and gulaman.
  7. Serve with ice.
